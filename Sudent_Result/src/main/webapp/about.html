About the Student Marks Upload System

This platform is developed to simplify the academic evaluation process in educational institutions. It allows faculty members to upload student marks securely and enables students to access their results online, instantly and conveniently.

Objectives

  • To digitize the student assessment process
  • To ensure fast and secure marks entry and retrieval
  • To reduce manual work and errors in academic result management
  • To provide seamless access to results for students anytime
